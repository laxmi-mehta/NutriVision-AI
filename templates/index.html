    Frequently asked questions

    What is NutriVision?

    Nutri Vision is a web application that helps users find out the calorie content and nutritional information of a dish by uploading a photo. It also provides detailed recipes and cooking instructions, making it easy for users to understand the nutritional aspects of their meals and find recipes for similar dishes.

    Is NutriVision a free calorie tracker app?

    Yes, Nutri Vision can be described as a free calorie tracker app. It allows users to upload photos of their meals to instantly receive information about the calorie content and nutritional details of the dishes. The app also provides recipes and cooking instructions, enhancing its utility as a dietary tool.

    What steps should I follow to get the nutrition data?

    To get nutrition data using Nutri Vision, simply open the app and upload a photo of the dish you want to analyze. The app will then process the image and provide you with detailed nutritional information, including calorie content. Additionally, you can explore recipes and cooking instructions related to the dish.

    Feel free to contact us and we'll help you out as soon as we can.

    What nutrition data can I track besides calories?

    Besides tracking calories, Nutri Vision can also provide information on protein, carbohydrates, fats, vitamins, minerals, and sodium. This helps you get a complete picture of the nutritional content of your dish.

    Can I save my favourite recipes and nutrition?

    Yes, Nutri Vision allows you to save your favorite recipes. Once you find a recipe you like, you can save it within the app for easy access later.

    Do I need to create an account to use Nutri Vision?

    No, creating an account is not required to use the basic features of Nutri Vision. However, signing up may unlock additional features and allow you to save your preferences and favorite recipes.
